**The Moonshade Family:**

Moonshade Digital began as a local record label formed through a partnership of 5 friends. 
Inclusivity has been at the forefront of our philosophy since our inception.
The label grew incrementally going on to reach a global audience thanks to the recent 
developments in social media and digital distribution. our team rapidly realized that the music landscape 
was changing irrevocably at an ever increasing rate. Moonshade Records, then Off Center Records, moved in a 
different direction creatively, taking on the role of a digital distributer. With releases such as Stoned In Public by
Chuckthespittah, Neoteric by Kuro Silence, and Blk|Nse by Rasiir, the labels initial albums paved the way for a new 
sound to emerge in the exploding underground. Challenging convention, these projects and others such as 
Vil.lian by Azyn Porter, Infundibulum by Kuro Silence introduced a spacey element that the current sound of music 
has adopted wholesale. 

___

## _Expansion & Development_

With collaborators going on to work with entities such as 10k Projects, Soulection, & EQ Distribution in partnership
with Roc Nation. Their prolific sound has gripped the industry and had a significant impact on the style & culture
of today's sonic landscape. From comics, to podcasts, brand deals, to virality, our 'local' label began to realize 
that we were doing a lot more than just making music, shooting music videos, and distributing. Hitting a stride in
the mass-content era we pivoted towards Tiktok as a pathway to growth. From 2018 through 2022 our label expanded into
a venture called Rappphouse Entertainment. With a new team Moonshade took a backseat and allowed Rappphouse to 
develop naturally. Unfortunately, the venture came together at the height of the 2020 pandemic and in 2022 the 
group was subsequently disbanded. The methodologies that had proven successful in in the label's early years 
evaporated in the wake of the content explosion experienced during the pandemic.

Having diverted our focus towards a artist development centered approach the incongruent cost benefit resulted
in significant losses and turbulence. Many of the shows planned for our Rappphouse artists were cancelled. We 
became entirely dependent on opm, streaming, sponsorships, and brand deals/collaborations for income. Our lineup 
was strong, but in the end the label could no longer sustain the venture. This lead to the shift towards 
Moonshade Media and Moonshade Digital. During the initial Rappphouse Ent. venture our team found great success in the 
realm of brand deals and collaborations. With the explosive growth we experienced with Kayrxn from TNGR, to 
FUN!FUN!FUN!, Control Freak, LYONSAULD, & The Roc'n Tac Connection we developed a blueprint for artist promotion
and sustainability in the new age. This blueprint wasn't as solidified as the team needed it to be and as such we've
researched the market and have been crafting a new roadmap to success.

## Background & Partnerships
From inception, as Off Center Records, to the partnership of OCAS - Off Center Alphasquad, Uptown Lowlife (ULL), 
to developing Moonshade Records, Rappphouse Ent., and Moonshade media we've helped shape the underground significantly.
That is to say nothing of the countless artists, managers, A&Rs, writers, filmographers, Djs, dancers, 
community & event organizers, and beyond. Especially to our supporters outside of the creator community. It is with
great thanks to everyone who's ever supported our efforts that we usher in the next stage of our progression in 
earnest.

As we continue to develop our digital experience, game development, videography expansion, and AI film ventures we 
intend on bringing all of this to life with our community, with all of you.

We've considered going public and listing our company on the stock 
exchange though there is a considerable level of growth needed to reach that goal; our greatest asset is our people.

on our first independent film as consultants and additional cameramen for *Collaborative Effort Productions:*

time to get our feet wet again.
